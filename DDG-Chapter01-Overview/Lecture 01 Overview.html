Apply 4 Formula in Curvature Flow

smooth setting

A simple version of this topic is curve shortening flow, where a closed curve moves in the normal direction with speed proportional to curvature.

3 key properties in curve shortening flow:

• (TOTAL) Total curvature remains constant throughout the flow. • (DRIFT) The center of mass does not drift from the origin. • (ROUND) Up to rescaling, the flow is stationary for circular curves.
